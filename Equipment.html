My Equipment

A1 mini

The Bambu Lab A1 Mini is a compact and smart FDM 3D printer with a build volume of 180 × 180 × 180 mm. It offers high-speed printing with vibration compensation, automatic bed leveling, and optional AMS Lite for multi-color printing. With quiet operation, built-in Wi-Fi, and seamless integration with Bambu Studio and the Handy app, it’s perfect for beginners and makers looking for fast, reliable results right out of the box.

Ender 3

The Creality Ender 3 is a budget-friendly FDM printer featuring a 220 × 220 × 250 mm build volume and a sturdy aluminum frame. Known for its open-source design and massive modding potential, it includes a heated bed, manual leveling, and reliable print quality, making it a favorite among hobbyists and tinkerers.

Ender 5 Pro

The Creality Ender 5 Pro builds on the Ender 3 with a cube-style frame for added stability and a taller 220 × 220 × 300 mm build volume. It comes with a silent mainboard, metal extruder, and Capricorn PTFE tubing for improved filament handling. It’s ideal for users seeking greater structural rigidity and taller prints while staying in the budget range.

3018 Prover V2

The SainSmart Genmitsu 3018-PROVer V2 is a compact desktop CNC router and laser engraver with a work area of 260 × 155 × 35 mm. Designed for cutting and engraving wood, acrylic, PCBs, and soft metals, it comes pre-assembled with GRBL control, USB connectivity, and an offline touchscreen controller. Its aluminum base provides enhanced stability, making it a great choice for precision CNC work on a budget.
