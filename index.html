About Me

I'm a 17-year-old developer and maker who discovered my passion for 3D printing when my nana got me a 3D printer for Christmas. That's how it all started! I love working with technology and 3D printers to create and fix things.

From fixing a knob for a speaker to working on CNC projects and modeling, I approach each project with creativity and technical curiosity. I'm currently working on converting a broken Ender 3 Pro into an Enderwire and making mods for my Ender 5 Pro.
