All About Zachary

Hi, I'm Zachary! I'm a passionate tech tinkerer who loves bringing ideas to life through coding and electronics. Whether I’m developing a website, solving problems with microcontrollers, or helping with lighting design at school, I’m always looking for ways to learn and improve. Technology is more than just a hobby for me — it's how I create, explore, and share what I love.
